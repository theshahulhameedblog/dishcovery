# Privacy Policy for DishcoveryNow

**Effective Date:** November 9, 2025
**Last Updated:** November 9, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to DishcoveryNow ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

## Information We Collect

### Camera and Photo Library Access
- **Camera Access**: DishcoveryNow requires camera access to capture photos of restaurant menus for translation purposes.
- **Photo Library Access**: The app may access your photo library to allow you to select previously taken menu photos.

### Menu Image Processing
- **Image Data**: When you capture or select a menu photo, the image is:
  - Temporarily compressed on your device
  - Sent to OpenAI's GPT-4 Vision API for menu analysis and translation
  - Not stored permanently on our servers
  - Processed according to OpenAI's data usage policies

### Data We DO NOT Collect
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers for advertising
- Browsing history or app usage analytics
- Any data from other apps on your device

## How We Use Your Information

The menu images you provide are used solely for:
1. **Menu Translation**: Extracting dish names and translating them to your preferred language
2. **Dish Information**: Providing descriptions, ingredients, allergen warnings, and nutritional data
3. **Image Recognition**: Processing images through OpenAI's GPT-4 Vision API

## Data Storage and Retention

### On Your Device
- The most recent menu translation result is cached locally on your device for quick access
- You can clear this data by closing and reopening the app
- App settings (language preferences) are stored locally

### On Our Servers
- We do **not** store menu images on our servers
- Images are processed in real-time and discarded immediately after analysis
- No personal data is retained

### Third-Party Services
- **OpenAI**: Menu images are sent to OpenAI's API for processing. OpenAI may temporarily cache data for up to 30 days for abuse prevention. See [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy)
- **Nutritionix/USDA**: Dish names are sent to nutrition APIs to fetch nutritional information (optional service)
- **Sentry**: We use Sentry for error monitoring. Only crash logs and error messages are collected (no personal data)

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **HTTPS Encryption**: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS encryption
- **No Account Required**: DishcoveryNow does not require account creation or login
- **Minimal Data Collection**: We only collect what's necessary for core functionality

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request information about data we process
- **Deletion**: Delete locally cached data by reinstalling the app
- **Opt-Out**: Stop using the app at any time
- **Control**: Revoke camera/photo permissions in iOS Settings

## Nutritional Information Disclaimer

### Data Sources and Accuracy

DishcoveryNow provides nutritional information sourced from third-party databases and AI-generated estimates. This information is provided for general informational and educational purposes only.

**Important Limitations:**
- Nutritional data is obtained from external sources including Nutritionix, USDA FoodData Central, and AI estimations

**Third-Party Data:**

- We do not independently verify the accuracy of third-party nutritional data

**EU Food Information Regulation (FIC) Compliance:**

**User Responsibility:**
