## About Dishcovery

Dishcovery is your personal culinary translator that helps you explore international cuisines with confidence. Simply snap a photo of any restaurant menu, and Dishcovery will instantly translate all dishes into your preferred language, complete with descriptions, ingredients, nutritional information, and allergen warnings.
